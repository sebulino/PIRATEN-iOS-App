## Context

What is the situation that forces a decision? Describe the forces at play:
technical constraints, organisational constraints, what will break if nothing
is decided. Be specific about *why now*.

List alternatives considered. For each, say briefly what is attractive about
it and why it was not chosen. Alternatives with no downsides are a warning
sign that the analysis is incomplete.

## Decision

State the decision in a single paragraph, in the imperative. "We will …" or
"The app uses …". Avoid conditionals. If the decision has sub-decisions,
enumerate them.

## Consequences

- **Positive.** What gets easier, safer, cheaper, faster.
- **Negative.** What gets harder, riskier, more expensive. This section is
  mandatory; a decision with no negatives has not been understood yet.
- **Follow-ups.** Anything this decision forces later — new ADRs, migration
  work, operational commitments, re-decision triggers.
